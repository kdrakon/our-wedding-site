The Ceremony

The Reception
Following the ceremony, a reception will be at:

Magenta Shores Golf & Country Club
1 Magenta Drive, Magenta NSW, Australia
Google it

A cocktail hour will start at 5:00 pm. Although we don't expect the ceremony to last until then, everyone is welcome to travel to the reception area soon after. There is a café located at the club where guests can rest and relax before the reception officially begins. A staff member of the club will direct you to the reception area from here.

Dinner, more drinks, dancing, and other activities will follow soon after the cocktail hour, so please make sure to have your travel arrangements from the ceremony and to your accommodation organised.
